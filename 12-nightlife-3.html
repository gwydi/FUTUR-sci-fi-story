Nightlife

The girl at the bar mixed me her recommended drink and handed it over with a sweet smile. The name of the clear mixture was lost to the void of all the things I can't remember. It tasted sour and sweet at the same time, and I felt glad to have trusted her with choosing my drink. After a few sips of it, the urge to move my body besides being completely exhausted overcame my consciousness. So, I downed the rest of it and with closed eyes moved to the rhythm of the electronical music that was taking control over my brain.
