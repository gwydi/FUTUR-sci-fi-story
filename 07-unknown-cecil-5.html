Inside Cecil Hotel

An elderly man was sitting behind the oak counter and greeted me friendly when I approached the reception. He seemed like a bubbly person, helpful and warm-hearted. We quickly exchanged a few words and even though I don’t see the meaning of small talk, I felt like a bit of warmth filled my heart. Slightly trembling he handed me the key to my room. Number 312. For a short moment I thought of my parents’ house, where I took off 2 weeks ago. Greenwood street 312. Without a second thought, I held the key a bit tighter, took my backpack off the ground and headed to the third floor.
