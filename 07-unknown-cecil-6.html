Inside Cecil Hotel

The hallway walls were covered in a beige wallpaper covered with a flower pattern, it looked old and tiring. I entered my room. To my unpleasant surprise, the carpet in my room had the same colour as the mentioned wallpaper. The walls also didn’t change. Beige in beige. If suddenly gravity were to leave this room, I wouldn’t be able to tell carpet from walls. The monotonous sound from the air conditioning machine matched the whole feel of the hotel room. I laid down on the bed, looked at the ceiling and sighed. As boring as this beige seemed, it was better than home. Excited yet exhausted I took a deep breath and thought about what to do next.
