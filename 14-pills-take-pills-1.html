The Room

I had to take the pills; it was not worth risking my wellbeing for, who knows what these people could do to me. I brought the hand that was already holding the pills ready up to my mouth and with a quick motion threw them in my mouth. They tasted sweet, just as I remembered them, but if you let them in your mouth for too long, the taste would turn bitter, and the mysterious people outside would know. With a gulp, I swallowed all three of them down and waited for the effect of tiredness to kick in. Preparedly I walked over to my bed and sat down on the extremely hard mattress. When I felt my head get dizzy, I laid down on my back and waited for sleep to kick in.
