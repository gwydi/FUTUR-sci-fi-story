Main Story

What I did know was that I took these pills every day. They made my body feel weak and my mind feel sleepy, like a sleeping pill that works within 2 minutes, I took them, and I fell asleep every time. I felt good when I took them, but I honestly had no clue what they really did to me. As I studied the pills, placing them in my palm and taking them close to my eyes, I wondered if I really needed them. My thoughts were interrupted by a knock on the door and an irritated voice telling me to take the pills, or measures would be taken. It was time to decide. Should I swallow the pills as I am told to fall into that mysterious sleep again, or muster up the courage and let the pills disappear somehow?
