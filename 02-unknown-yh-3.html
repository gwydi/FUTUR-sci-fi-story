Main Story

It was like taking a step into the last decade as I entered the youth hostel. The walls were pale, old wooden furniture was cramped in the tiny entrance room, that was used both as a lobby and a community room at once. Three young-looking people were playing pool in a corner, while two older, touristy-looking men were loudly arguing about their plans for the next day over a beer. They were sat at a table in the other corner by the window.
