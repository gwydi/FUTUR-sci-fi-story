In the youth hostel

The group consisted of two taller men who I assumed were in their early twenties and a woman about the same age. All had blonde hair and a shiny attitude that must attract people. I thanked the receptionist for her help and decided to take a leap and approach the group. They told me about their stay here. Two of them were from a Nordic European country I can't recall the name of, the other one is from the city. I was surprised that the two foreigners were able to be here. For months all the news talked about was the devastation the Masquis caused in Europe. They started destroying anything that was in their way, leaving ruins and dried up vegetation behind, so bad that most parts were now inhabitable.
